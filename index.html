About Maud

Voiceover in four
languages.

I'm Maud Fasel — a multilingual voiceover artist based in Lausanne, working in English, French, German and Italian. Over the years I've had the opportunity to work for Swiss brands across retail, finance, luxury and the public sector.

I work across radio and TV commercials, corporate films and brand narratives, bringing care and consistency to every project. I record from my home studio in Lausanne and work regularly with Arcane Productions in Vevey.
